Cookie Policy

Last updated: August 15, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They help the Site remember your actions and preferences.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Strictly necessary: site performance, security, and core features.
  • Preferences: remember choices (e.g., theme or language).
  • Analytics: understand how visitors use the Site (aggregate data).
  • Advertising: deliver and measure ads (including personalized ads where allowed).

3. Third-Party Cookies

Third parties (e.g., Google) may set cookies for analytics and advertising. See Google’s policy: partner-sites.

4. Your Choices

  • Control cookies via your browser (block or delete).
  • Opt-out of personalized ads: Google Ads Settings, aboutads.info/choices, NAI.
  • For EEA/UK, use the consent banner to accept or reject non-essential cookies.

5. Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy. Updates appear here with a new date.
