ABOUT ME

My name is Anas HAMOUTNI, and I am an Actuary and an AI enthusiast from Morocco. I hold an engineering degree from the National Institute of Statistics and Applied Economics (INSEA) in Rabat.

Ever since I was young, I've been drawn to solving puzzles, and this same passion drives my approach to tackling every data problem that I encounter. I find great satisfaction in discovering new patterns and devising creative solutions, which has always been my primary motivation.

With over 3 years of experience, primarily in the insurance industry, I've had the opportunity to experiment with numerous ideas and develop innovative solutions that are ready for use. Through these experiences, I have enhanced my analytical and problem-solving skills, honed my social abilities, and strengthened my capacity for teamwork.

Kudos AI is where I document those lessons in public. The blog hosts the full write-ups, while the accompanying GitHub repositories and Google Colab notebooks let readers validate every project I discuss.

What you’ll find on my LinkedIn & Kudos AI

  • Applied statistics and machine learning projects for insurers, financial services, and operations teams.
  • Hands-on experimentation shared through the Kudos AI blog, Kaggle notebooks, and project write-ups.
  • Consulting engagements where I scope initiatives, build reproducible assets, and transfer knowledge to in-house teams.
